Mexican Pasta

mexican chicken pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 1 diced chicken breast
  • 1 pound pasta noodle
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. cook chicken in a large skillet
  2. remove chicken and cook onion and bell pepper in same skillet
  3. add chicken, heavy cream, cheese, and cooked pasta to pan
  4. mix and enjoy
