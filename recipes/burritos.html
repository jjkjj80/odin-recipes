Burritos

Burrito

Ingredients

  • burrito shells
  • 1 pound of ground hamburger
  • 2 tablespoons of taco sauce
  • taco seasoning
  • mexican rice
  • sour cream

Directions

  1. cook hamburger in a large skillet adding taco seasoning and taco sauce
  2. in a medium pot, cook the mexican rice
  3. when both are done, add hamburger, rice, and sourcream to a burrito shell
  4. fold the side of the shell in then roll the shell to create the burrito shape
