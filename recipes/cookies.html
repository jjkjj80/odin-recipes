Peanut butter cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl untill well mixed
  3. ball of mix in your hands into roughly 1 inch balls and lay them out on a cookie sheet
  4. using a fork, press down on the balls in two directions to flatten them and give them a cross design
  5. sprinkle a pinch of sugar onto all of the cookies
  6. stick in the oven for 10-12 minutes
  7. remove from the oven and allow to cool before enjoying!
